<p><p>The road marking coatings market comprises several types, each serving distinct needs. Thermoplastic marking coatings are durable and heat-applied, ideal for high-traffic areas. Water-based marking coatings are eco-friendly, offering quick drying times and easy cleanup. Solvent-based marking coatings provide strong adhesion and are suitable for varied surfaces, though they may emit VOCs. Two-component road marking coatings combine resins and hardeners for superior performance and longevity, making them suitable for demanding environments and enhancing visibility.</p></p>
