<p><p>The Spray Booth Protective Film market consists of two primary types: solid paint resistant masking film and liquid paint resistant masking film. Solid paint resistant masking film is designed for applications where dry paint overspray occurs, providing a durable barrier against solid particles. In contrast, liquid paint resistant masking film is intended for environments with wet spray applications, effectively preventing liquid paint from adhering to surfaces. Both films enhance efficiency and protect equipment and surfaces during painting processes in spray booths.</p></p>
