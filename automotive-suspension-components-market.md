<p><p>The automotive suspension components market encompasses various types that enhance vehicle stability and comfort. Coil springs, designed to absorb shock, are widely used for their flexibility. Leaf springs offer durability and strength, often found in heavier vehicles. Stabilizer bars minimize body roll during turns, improving handling. Suspension arms connect the wheel assembly to the chassis, allowing for better wheel alignment and motion control. Other components, including shock absorbers and bushings, also play vital roles in optimizing ride quality and safety.</p></p>
