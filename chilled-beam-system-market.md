<p><p>Chilled beam systems are advanced HVAC solutions that use water-circulated beams to cool or heat spaces efficiently. The market is segmented into three types: concealed, exposed, and recessed. Concealed beams are hidden within the ceiling, providing a clean aesthetic while maintaining climate control. Exposed beams are visible, often used for industrial or modern designs, adding visual interest. Recessed beams integrate seamlessly into ceilings, offering a minimalistic look with effective temperature regulation, appealing to both residential and commercial sectors.</p></p>
