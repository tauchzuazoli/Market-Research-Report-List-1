<p><p>The wireless endoscopes market is segmented into two main types: fixed focus and zoom focus. Fixed focus endoscopes have a predetermined focal length, providing a consistent view ideal for specific procedures, often making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, zoom focus endoscopes offer adjustable focal lengths, allowing for versatile imaging capabilities and enhanced detail during examinations. This flexibility makes zoom focus endoscopes suitable for a wider range of applications, catering to diverse medical needs in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
