<p><p>The low emission vehicle market encompasses several types aimed at reducing environmental impact. Mild Hybrid Electric Vehicles (MHEVs) use a small electric motor to assist the internal combustion engine, enhancing fuel efficiency. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine a conventional engine with a battery-powered electric motor for improved efficiency and reduced emissions. Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) can be charged from an external source and offer extended electric-only driving ranges. Pure Electric Vehicles (EVs) operate entirely on electricity, producing zero tailpipe emissions and promoting clean energy use.</p></p>
