<p><p>Smart medication inhalers are advanced devices designed to improve the management of respiratory conditions. The market is primarily categorized into three types: Metered-Dose Inhalers (MDIs), which deliver a specific amount of medication as a mist; Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs), which provide medication in a dry powder form that patients inhale; and Propellant-Free Multi-Dose Spray Inhalers, which use a unique mechanism to dispense medication without traditional propellants. These innovations aim to enhance adherence while monitoring patient usage and effectiveness.</p></p>
