<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are critical components in modern air conditioning systems, regulating refrigerant flow with precision. There are two main types: Electromagnetic EEVs, which use an electromagnetic coil to control the valve opening, allowing for quick adjustments in refrigerant flow based on system demand. Electric EEVs, on the other hand, utilize electric motors for more refined control and efficiency, enabling smoother operation and better energy management. Both types enhance system performance and energy efficiency in HVAC applications.</p></p>
