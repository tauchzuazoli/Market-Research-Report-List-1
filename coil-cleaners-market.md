<p><ul><li>Acid-Based Cleaners</li><li>Non-Acid Cleaners</li><li>Self-Rinsing Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coil cleaners market is segmented into three main types: acid-based cleaners, non-acid cleaners, and self-rinsing cleaners. Acid-based cleaners are effective at removing tough scale and mineral deposits but must be used carefully due to their corrosive nature. Non-acid cleaners are milder, making them suitable for regular maintenance without damaging the coils. Self-rinsing cleaners offer the convenience of removing dirt and grime without the need for manual rinsing, utilizing surfactants that dislodge debris and allow it to wash away easily.</p></p>
