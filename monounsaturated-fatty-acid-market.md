<p><p>The monounsaturated fatty acid market consists of three main types based on their chemical structures: ω-3, ω-6, and ω-9 fatty acids. ω-3 fatty acids are essential fats found in fish oils and flaxseeds, known for their heart-health benefits. ω-6 fatty acids, primarily sourced from vegetable oils, play a crucial role in cellular functions but should be consumed in balance with ω-3s. ω-9 fatty acids, such as oleic acid from olive oil, are non-essential and support cardiovascular health, contributing to the overall market diversity.</p></p>
