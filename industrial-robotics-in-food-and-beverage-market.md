<p><p>Industrial robotics in the food and beverage market encompasses various robot types, each suited for specific tasks. </p><p>Articulated robots feature rotating joints, ideal for complex movements like packaging. Cartesian robots use linear movements, perfect for precise pick-and-place operations. SCARA robots excel in assembly tasks due to their rigid structure. Cylindrical robots offer vertical and horizontal movement, suited for handling containers. Parallel robots provide high-speed and precise operations, ideal for bottling or sorting. Other market types include delta robots and mobile robots, enhancing versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
