<p><strong>The Polymeric Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solution-Diffusion Membrane</li><li>Ion Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric membrane market encompasses various types, primarily including solution-diffusion membranes and ion exchange membranes. Solution-diffusion membranes operate by allowing selective permeation of gases or liquids through a polymer matrix, driven by concentration gradients. In contrast, ion exchange membranes facilitate the movement of ions while preventing the passage of neutral molecules, making them critical for applications like water purification and fuel cells. Both types are integral to enhancing separation processes in various industries, including environmental and energy sectors.</p></p>
