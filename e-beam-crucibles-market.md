<p><p>E-Beam crucibles are essential in the semiconductor and materials industries for melting and refining metals. The market is divided into round and cone types, catering to different processing needs. Round crucibles are typically used for uniform heating and are ideal for larger volumes, ensuring consistent material quality. In contrast, cone-shaped crucibles are designed for efficient material drainage and easier pouring, making them suitable for applications requiring precise control over melted substances. Both types enhance process efficiency and output.</p></p>
