<p><p>The automotive brake booster market is segmented into single diaphragm boosters, dual diaphragm boosters, and others. Single diaphragm boosters utilize a single flexible diaphragm to amplify brake pedal force, suitable for compact vehicle designs where space is limited. Dual diaphragm boosters, featuring two diaphragms, provide greater brake assistance and responsiveness, making them preferable for larger and higher-performance vehicles. The "others" category includes innovative designs and emerging technologies, catering to specific applications and advancements in braking systems, enhancing overall vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
