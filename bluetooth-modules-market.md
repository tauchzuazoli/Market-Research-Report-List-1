<p><p>The Bluetooth modules market comprises three main types: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) modules, Dual-mode Bluetooth modules, and Classic Bluetooth modules. BLE modules prioritize energy efficiency, making them ideal for IoT devices and wearables that require long battery life. Dual-mode modules support both BLE and Classic Bluetooth, facilitating compatibility across a broader range of devices. Classic Bluetooth modules cater to traditional applications requiring higher data transfer rates, such as audio streaming and larger file transfers, ensuring diverse connectivity solutions.</p></p>
