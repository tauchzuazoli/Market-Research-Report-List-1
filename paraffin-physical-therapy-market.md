<p><p>The paraffin physical therapy market is divided into two main types: ready-to-use paraffin and ready-to-heat paraffin. In contrast, ready-to-heat paraffin requires heating before use, allowing for customizable temperature control tailored to individual therapy needs. Both types are utilized for their therapeutic benefits in treating conditions like arthritis and muscle stiffness, providing relief and enhancing recovery.</p></p>
