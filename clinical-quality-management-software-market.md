<p><p>Clinical Quality Management Software (CQMS) is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions are installed directly on a healthcare provider's infrastructure, offering enhanced control over data security and compliance, but may require significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based CQMS operates via the internet, allowing for easier access, scalability, and reduced IT overhead. It facilitates real-time data sharing and collaboration but may raise concerns about data privacy and reliance on internet connectivity.</p></p>
