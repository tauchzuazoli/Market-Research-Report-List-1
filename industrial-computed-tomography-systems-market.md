<p><p>Industrial Computed Tomography (CT) systems are categorized into three main types based on their energy levels and applications. High Energy Industrial CT utilizes X-rays with high energy to penetrate dense materials, suitable for examining large and thick objects. Low Energy Industrial CT, on the other hand, employs lower energy levels ideal for inspecting smaller or less dense components. Mini-Focus Industrial CT systems feature a compact design, providing localized imaging for precision measurement and defect detection in intricate parts and assemblies.</p></p>
