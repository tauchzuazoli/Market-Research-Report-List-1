<p><ul><li>Closed-Loop Current Sensors</li><li>Open Loop Current Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC current sensor market is primarily divided into two types: closed-loop and open-loop current sensors. Closed-loop sensors utilize a feedback mechanism, providing high accuracy and minimal external interference, making them suitable for precision applications. In contrast, open-loop sensors are simpler and often more cost-effective, measuring current without feedback, resulting in less accuracy but broader usability. Both types serve various sectors including industrial, automotive, and renewable energy, addressing diverse current measurement needs with distinct advantages.</p></p>
