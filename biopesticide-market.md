<p><strong>The Biopesticide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbial Pesticides</li><li>Biochemical Pesticides</li><li>Plant-Incorporated Protectants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopesticide market consists of several key types. Microbial pesticides utilize naturally occurring microorganisms, such as bacteria or fungi, to control pests. Biochemical pesticides are derived from natural materials, such as plant extracts or minerals, and disrupt pest life cycles. Plant-incorporated protectants (PIPs) involve genetically engineered plants that produce pest-resistant proteins. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses various unconventional biopesticides, including organic formulations and alternative pest management solutions. Together, these categories promote sustainable agriculture by reducing chemical pesticide usage.</p></p>
