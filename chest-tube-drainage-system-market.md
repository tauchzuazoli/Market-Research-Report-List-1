<p><p>The chest tube drainage system market can be categorized into single chamber and multiple chamber systems. Single chamber systems consist of a simple reservoir that collects fluid and air, offering a straightforward approach for basic procedures. In contrast, multiple chamber systems include separate compartments for collection, water seal, and suction, allowing for more complex management of pleural effusions or pneumothorax. Multiple chamber systems provide enhanced functionality and monitoring capabilities, catering to diverse clinical needs and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
