<p><p>The selfie stick market comprises three main types: Remote-Triggered Selfie Sticks, Wired Selfie Sticks, and Bluetooth Selfie Sticks. Remote-Triggered Selfie Sticks utilize a remote control, allowing users to take photos from a distance without needing to touch the stick. Wired Selfie Sticks connect directly to a smartphone's headphone jack for instant photo capturing, offering a simple, battery-free solution. Bluetooth Selfie Sticks connect wirelessly to devices, providing flexibility and ease of use, often with additional features like adjustable angles.</p></p>
