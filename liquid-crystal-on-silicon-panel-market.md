<p><p>The Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) panel market features two primary designs: three-panel and one-panel systems. Three-panel designs utilize separate panels for red, green, and blue colors, offering superior color accuracy and brightness, making them ideal for high-end projectors and displays. In contrast, one-panel designs integrate the color processing onto a single panel, resulting in a more compact system. While one-panel designs are often more cost-effective and suitable for portable applications, they may compromise on color quality and brightness compared to their three-panel counterparts.</p></p>
