<p><strong>The Angiography Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography Devices</li><li>CT Angiography Devices</li><li>MR Angiography Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiography devices market encompasses three primary types: X-Ray angiography devices, CT angiography devices, and MR angiography devices. X-Ray angiography uses radiation to visualize blood vessels, providing real-time images for diagnosing cardiovascular issues. CT angiography enhances X-ray images with computed tomography for detailed vessel assessment. MR angiography employs magnetic resonance imaging to generate high-quality images without ionizing radiation, making it suitable for soft tissue evaluation. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, contributing to advancements in vascular imaging and diagnostics.</p></p>
