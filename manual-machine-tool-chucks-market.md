<p><p>The manual machine tool chucks market is categorized into three main types: 2-jaw, 3-jaw, and 4-jaw chucks. </p><p>2-jaw chucks are primarily used for gripping flat or square workpieces, providing stability and precise alignment. 3-jaw chucks are more common, offering versatility for cylindrical workpieces by automatically centering them when clamped. </p><p>4-jaw chucks allow for even greater precision and flexibility, accommodating irregularly shaped items and enabling independent adjustment of each jaw for enhanced accuracy in machining processes.</p></p>
