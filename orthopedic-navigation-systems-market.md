<p><strong>The Orthopedic Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT-Free Navigation Systems</li><li>Image-Free Navigation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedic navigation systems enhance surgical precision by providing real-time guidance during procedures. CT-Free Navigation Systems utilize pre-operative imaging and advanced algorithms to offer intraoperative guidance without the need for continuous imaging, allowing for streamlined workflows. On the other hand, Image-Free Navigation Systems operate without any pre-operative scans, relying on anatomical landmarks and real-time tracking to guide surgeons during interventions. Both types aim to improve surgical accuracy, reduce complications, and facilitate better patient outcomes in orthopedic surgeries.</p></p>
