The market for SMES is divided into two types: Low Temperature SMES, which operates near absolute zero using liquid helium, offering high efficiency but higher costs; and High Temperature SMES, which utilizes materials that function at higher temperatures, such as liquid nitrogen, making them more cost-effective and easier to maintain, thus expanding their application potential.</p></p>
