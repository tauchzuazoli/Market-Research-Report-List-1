<p><p>The aerobic septic system market comprises several types, including Conventional Activated Sludge, Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR), and Membrane Bioreactor (MBR). Conventional Activated Sludge uses aeration tanks for organic matter degradation. MBBR utilizes moving biofilm carriers for enhanced efficiency and compact design. MBR integrates membrane filtration with biological treatment, offering superior effluent quality and space efficiency. Each system caters to various wastewater treatment needs, focusing on environmental sustainability and operational efficiency.</p></p>
