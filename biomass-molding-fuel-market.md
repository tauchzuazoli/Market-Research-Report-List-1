<p><p>The biomass molding fuel market consists of two primary types: granular and block fuels. Granular biomass fuel is typically produced in pellet form, offering high energy density and ease of transport, ideal for residential and industrial heating. Block biomass fuel, on the other hand, is formed into larger shapes such as logs or bricks, often used for stoves and fireplaces. Both types serve as renewable energy sources, promoting sustainability and reducing reliance on fossil fuels while catering to various energy needs.</p></p>
