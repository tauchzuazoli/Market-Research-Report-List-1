<p><p>The Portable Medical Ventilators market comprises two key types: Manual and Mechanical Ventilators. Manual ventilators, often known as bag-valve-mask systems, require healthcare professionals to operate them, making them suitable for emergency situations and transport. In contrast, Mechanical ventilators are automated devices that provide continuous airflow and precise control over ventilation settings, catering to patients with chronic respiratory conditions. Both types are essential in various medical settings, including hospitals, ambulances, and home care, ensuring effective respiratory support.</p></p>
