<p><p>Antibody engineering services encompass various techniques, including gene synthesis, transient expression, isotype switching, and chimerization. Gene synthesis involves creating tailored DNA sequences for specific antibodies. Transient expression refers to rapid antibody production in host cells without stable integration. Isotype switching modifies antibody classes to enhance efficacy and specificity. Chimerization combines elements from different species to generate hybrid antibodies, improving therapeutic potential. These market segments cater to diverse research and clinical applications, driving advancements in biotechnology and therapeutics.</p></p>
