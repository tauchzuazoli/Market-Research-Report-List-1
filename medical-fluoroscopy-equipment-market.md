<p><ul><li>Fixed Fluoroscopy Equipment</li><li>C-Arms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical fluoroscopy equipment market comprises two primary types: fixed fluoroscopy equipment and C-Arms. Fixed fluoroscopy systems are stationary setups used in operating rooms and radiology departments for continuous imaging during complex procedures, providing high-resolution images for accurate diagnosis and treatment. On the other hand, C-Arms are portable fluoroscopic devices that offer flexibility for use in various clinical settings, including surgical suites and outpatient facilities, allowing real-time visualization of anatomy during interventions. Both types enhance procedural efficiency and patient outcomes.</p></p>
