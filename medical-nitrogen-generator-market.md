<p><strong>The Medical Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSA</li><li>CMS</li><li>Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nitrogen generator market consists of three main types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption), CMS (Carbon Molecular Sieves), and Membrane technologies. PSA uses alternating pressure to separate nitrogen from other gases, producing high-purity nitrogen. CMS employs carbon sieves to selectively adsorb unwanted gases, enhancing nitrogen purity. Membrane technology relies on selective permeability of membranes to separate nitrogen from air, offering a compact solution. Each type serves different medical applications, providing efficient nitrogen generation tailored to healthcare needs.</p></p>
