<p><strong>The Unsaturated Polyester Molding Compounds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sheet Molding Compounds</li><li>Bulk Molding Compounds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unsaturated Polyester Molding Compounds (UPMC) encompass two primary types: Sheet Molding Compounds (SMC) and Bulk Molding Compounds (BMC). SMC is a pre-impregnated composite material, typically used for producing large, rigid parts through compression molding, offering excellent surface finish and structural integrity. BMC, on the other hand, consists of a mix of polyester resin, fillers, and reinforcement fibers, suitable for intricate shapes through injection molding, providing high strength and versatility for smaller components in automotive and electrical applications.</p></p>
