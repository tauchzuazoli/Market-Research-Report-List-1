<p><strong>The Travel Mobility Scooter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class 2 Scooter</li><li>Class 3 Scooter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Travel mobility scooters are categorized into Class 2 and Class 3 scooters, reflecting their intended use and legal restrictions. Class 2 scooters, designed for pavement use, typically have lower speeds (up to 4 mph) and are compact for easy maneuverability in tight spaces. Conversely, Class 3 scooters can be used on roads, reaching speeds up to 8 mph, and offer enhanced safety features. Both types cater to individuals with mobility challenges, promoting independence and ease of travel.</p></p>
