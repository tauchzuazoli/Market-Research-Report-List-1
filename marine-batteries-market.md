<p><strong>The Marine Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Battery</li><li>Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine batteries market comprises three main types: Gel batteries, Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries, and Flooded batteries. Gel batteries use a gelled electrolyte, offering deep discharge capabilities and resistance to vibration, making them ideal for marine applications. AGM batteries are maintenance-free, provide high discharge rates, and excel in safety, suitable for a variety of marine uses. Flooded batteries are traditional lead-acid batteries, cost-effective and reliable, but require maintenance, making them less popular in modern marine settings.</p></p>
