<p><ul><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller System</li><li>Varying Pitch Propeller System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft propeller systems market comprises two main types: fixed pitch and varying pitch propeller systems. Fixed pitch propellers have blades set at a specific angle, offering simplicity and reliability, primarily used in smaller aircraft. In contrast, varying pitch propellers allow for blade angle adjustments during flight, providing enhanced performance, fuel efficiency, and better control, making them suitable for larger and more advanced aircraft. Both types cater to different operational needs and aircraft specifications in the aviation industry.</p></p>
