<p><p>Tunable dye lasers can be categorized into continuous and pulsed markets based on their operational modes. Continuous tunable dye lasers emit a constant beam of light, offering stable output and suitable for applications like spectroscopy and medical diagnostics. In contrast, pulsed tunable dye lasers release light in short bursts, which allows for high peak power and is ideal for applications such as laser ablation, precision cutting, and research requiring detailed temporal resolution. Each market serves distinct scientific and industrial needs.</p></p>
