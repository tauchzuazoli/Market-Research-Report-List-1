<p><p>The radiation curing adhesive market comprises two main types: ultraviolet (UV) curing and electron beam (EB) curing. UV curing uses ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, allowing for quick drying and strong adhesion, making it ideal for coatings, inks, and laminates. Electron beam curing, on the other hand, employs high-energy electron beams to achieve instant curing, providing deeper penetration and enhanced durability. Both methods offer efficient, environmentally friendly solutions with minimal solvents, meeting the demands of various industrial applications.</p></p>
