<p><strong>The Time of flight ToF Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect</li><li>Direct</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Time of Flight (ToF) chip market is primarily divided into two types: indirect and direct. Indirect ToF uses modulated light, calculating distance by measuring the phase shift in reflected light. This method is often more accurate over longer distances but can be slower. Conversely, direct ToF measures the time it takes for a light pulse to travel to an object and back, providing rapid distance measurements ideal for applications like gesture recognition and 3D mapping.</p></p>
