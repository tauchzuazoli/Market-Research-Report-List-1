<p><strong>The Valves and Controls Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sliding-Stem Globe</li><li>V-notch Ball</li><li>Butterfly Types</li><li>Angle Types</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The valves and controls market encompasses various types based on design and functionality. Sliding-stem globe valves regulate flow with a movable stem, while V-notch ball valves offer precise control, particularly in throttling applications. Butterfly valves are compact, allowing for quick shut-off in larger pipelines. Angle valves facilitate flow in a specific direction, reducing turbulence. Other types include check valves, pressure relief valves, and solenoid valves, each serving unique applications across industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
